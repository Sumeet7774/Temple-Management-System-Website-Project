About Sarangpur Kashtabhanjan Dev Mandir

The Sarangpur Kashtabhanjan Dev Mandir is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located in the serene village of Sarangpur, near Botad in Gujarat, India. This temple is renowned for its spiritual significance and attracts devotees from across the country and around the world.

The temple was established in the early 20th century by the revered saint Shastriji Maharaj of the Swaminarayan Sampradaya. It is one of the most prominent and historic temples of the Swaminarayan sect, known for its miraculous powers to alleviate the sufferings and sorrows of devotees.

The main deity of the temple, Kashtabhanjan Hanuman, is depicted in a powerful and dynamic form, symbolizing the removal of all hardships and obstacles. The temple complex is an architectural marvel, combining traditional craftsmanship with spiritual tranquility.

Visitors to the Sarangpur Kashtabhanjan Dev Mandir can participate in various religious ceremonies and rituals, including the daily aarti and darshan of the deity. The temple also offers prasadam and has facilities for devotees to stay and meditate in the peaceful environment.

The temple's serene surroundings and the powerful presence of Lord Hanuman make it a perfect place for spiritual seekers and those looking to find solace and strength in their lives. The Sarangpur Kashtabhanjan Dev Mandir continues to be a beacon of faith and devotion, inspiring countless devotees to lead a life of righteousness and devotion.
